The Problem

Patients leave the ER confused and at risk

Complex medical language creates a dangerous gap between what doctors write and what patients understand — with real clinical and economic consequences.

📄
88%

Incomprehensible notes

Up to 88% of discharge instructions use technical jargon beyond the general population’s health literacy level, leaving patients unable to follow care plans.

🔄
↑30%

Higher readmissions

Language discordance is associated with higher 30-day readmission rates. Poor comprehension drives avoidable ER revisits and increased healthcare spending.

⏱
1–5 days

Translation delays

Professional human translation of discharge documents can take 1–5 days, or be deferred entirely due to cost constraints, leaving patients without timely guidance.

Our Solution

AI that speaks the patient’s language

EDxPlain automatically transforms technical discharge notes into structured, clinically validated, patient-friendly explanations in real time.

1

7-Section Structured Output

Diagnosis, procedures, home care, medications, red flags, follow-up, and confidence indicators — each designed for actionable patient comprehension.

2

Protocol-Anchored (RAG)

Augmented with institutional clinical protocols (CERCA’M) via Retrieval-Augmented Generation, ensuring recommendations align with hospital guidelines.

3

Bilingual & Adaptive

Natively handles Catalan and Spanish with gender-adaptive phrasing. Designed for linguistic scalability to other European languages.

4

Readability Validated

Every output scored with Fernández-Huerta readability index (target ≥ 60, ~6th grade). Quantified comprehension, not just translation.

How It Works

An end-to-end AWS Bedrock pipeline from clinical note to patient-ready document — fully auditable.

1
📥

Ingest

Discharge note ingested from EHR / clinical system (S3 or API integration).

2
🔍

Pathology detection + institutional protocols (CERCA’M) retrieval via Bedrock Knowledge Base RAG.

3
🤖

Claude Sonnet on AWS Bedrock transforms the note. Closed-context, temperature = 0, deterministic.

4
✅

Validate

4-judge LLM evaluation + readability scoring + clinician approval before delivery.

Clinical Safety

Defense in Depth

Multiple safeguards ensure clinical accuracy and patient safety at every pipeline step.

🔒

Closed-Context AI

The LLM uses only information from the original discharge note. No external knowledge, no hallucination from web data. Temperature = 0 for deterministic output.

⚖️

4-Judge Evaluation

Four independent LLM judges verify coverage, fidelity, critical values, and completeness. A critical judge blocks any dosage discrepancies.

👨‍⚕️

Human-in-the-Loop

Every explanation is a draft. The attending physician reviews and approves before patient delivery. AI assists — never replaces clinical judgment.

B2B SaaS for Hospitals & Health Networks

EDxPlain is a plug-and-play API that integrates into existing EHR systems via HL7/FHIR standards. When the physician signs off a discharge note, the patient-friendly version generates in the background — no new workflows, no new logins. Each execution produces a full audit trail for compliance teams.

The architecture adapts to any hospital by swapping the RAG Knowledge Base to local institutional protocols — turning generic AI into hospital-specific, guideline-anchored patient communication.
