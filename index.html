The Problem

Patients leave the ER confused

Complex medical language creates a dangerous gap between what doctors write and what patients understand.

📄
88%

Incomprehensible notes

Up to 88% of discharge instructions use medical jargon that the general population cannot understand, leaving patients confused and anxious.

🔄
↑

Avoidable readmissions

Poor understanding leads to lower treatment adherence, missed follow-ups, and preventable ER revisits that strain healthcare systems.

⏱️
0 min

No time to explain

Emergency physicians face extreme time pressure. There's no scalable way to personalize explanations for every patient at discharge.

Our Solution

AI that speaks the patient's language

EDxPlain automatically transforms technical discharge notes into structured, clinically validated, patient-friendly explanations.

1

Structured output

Seven clear sections: diagnosis, procedures, home care, medications, red flags, follow-up, and confidence indicators.

2

Protocol-anchored (RAG)

Augmented with institutional clinical protocols (CERCA'M) via Retrieval-Augmented Generation, ensuring recommendations align with hospital guidelines.

3

Bilingual support

Natively handles Catalan and Spanish discharge notes, adapting the output language and gender-aware phrasing.

4

Readability validated

Every output is scored with the Fernández-Huerta readability index, targeting ≥ 60 (6th grade level) for true comprehension.

Pipeline

How It Works

An end-to-end AWS-powered pipeline from clinical note to patient-ready document.

1
📥

Ingest

Discharge note is read from the clinical system (S3 or local provider).

2
🔍

Enrich

Pathology detection + CERCA'M template retrieval via RAG (Bedrock Knowledge Base).

3
🤖

Generate

Claude Sonnet on AWS Bedrock transforms the note with closed-context prompting (temp = 0).

4
✅

Validate

4-judge LLM evaluation + readability scoring. Clinician reviews and approves before delivery.

Clinical Safety

Defense in Depth

Multiple layers of safeguards ensure clinical accuracy and patient safety at every step.

🔒

Closed-Context AI

The LLM can only use information from the original discharge note. No external knowledge, no hallucination risk from web data. Temperature = 0 for deterministic output.

⚖️

4-Judge Evaluation

Four independent LLM judges verify coverage, fidelity, critical values, and completeness. A critical judge can block any output with dosage discrepancies.

👨‍⚕️

Human-in-the-Loop

Every generated explanation is a draft. The attending physician always reviews and approves before it reaches the patient. AI assists, never replaces.
